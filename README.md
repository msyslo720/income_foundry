# The Income Foundry

> **Forging Reliable Income**

---

## Overview

The Income Foundry is a documented operating system for building and managing a scalable options income business.

Rather than treating options trading as a collection of individual trades, The Income Foundry approaches it as a manufacturing business built upon repeatable processes, disciplined risk management, and continuous improvement.

The project combines investment philosophy, business governance, operational procedures, performance measurement, and software engineering principles into a single, cohesive operating system.

---

## Mission

> Build a scalable options income business that generates reliable cash flow through disciplined option selling while preserving capital and continuously improving the system that produces it.

---

## Philosophy

The Income Foundry is founded on several core beliefs:

* Process is more important than individual outcomes.
* Capital preservation comes before return maximization.
* Every production line exists for a defined purpose.
* Premium is earned—not chased.
* Assignment is a normal part of the manufacturing process.
* Continuous improvement compounds over time.

---

## The Foundry Operating System (FOS)

The repository is organized around the Foundry Operating System.

The FOS consists of five integrated systems:

* Governance
* Asset Management
* Operations
* Performance
* Knowledge Management

Together they create a repeatable operating system capable of supporting an options income business from a single production line to a mature retirement portfolio.
