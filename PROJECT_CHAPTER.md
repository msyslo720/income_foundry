# Project Charter

## Project Name

The Income Foundry

---

## Purpose

The purpose of this repository is to develop, document, and continuously improve a complete operating system for managing an options income business.

The repository is intended to become the single source of truth for all business policies, operating procedures, performance metrics, and supporting tools.

---

## Vision

To build an operating system that enables disciplined, low-maintenance management of an options income business capable of operating consistently throughout retirement.

---

## Scope

Included:

* Business philosophy
* Investment governance
* Standard Operating Procedures
* Asset evaluation framework
* Performance measurement
* Capital allocation
* Production line management
* Continuous improvement
* Dashboard design
* Workbook development
* Documentation standards

Excluded:

* Day trading strategies
* High-frequency trading
* Speculative trading systems
* Market prediction systems
* Unmanaged discretionary trading

---

## Success Criteria

The project is successful when:

* Every recurring business process is documented.
* Every important decision is governed by an approved procedure.
* Performance can be objectively measured.
* The business can operate with approximately one hour of management per week.
* The documentation enables another qualified operator to manage the Foundry consistently.

---

## Guiding Values

* Discipline
* Consistency
* Stewardship
* Continuous Improvement
* Professionalism
* Capital Preservation

---

## Definition of Done

A document is considered complete when:

* It has a unique document identifier.
* Metadata is complete.
* Cross references are included.
* Examples are provided where appropriate.
* Failure modes are documented.
* Related checklists exist.
* Revision history has been updated.
* It has been reviewed and approved.

---

## Long-Term Vision

The repository will eventually support:

* A professionally published operations manual
* A searchable documentation website
* A KPI workbook
* A Foundry Dashboard
* An AI-assisted Investment Committee
* Continuous operational improvement through versioned documentation
